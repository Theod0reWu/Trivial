How To Play

First host or join a game

To host a game:
  1. Enter your username
  2. Click HOST GAME
  3. Click on the room code to copy it
  4. Share the room code with your friends!
To join a game:
  1. Enter your username
  2. Click JOIN GAME
  3. Enter the room code of an existing room
  4. Click JOIN!

On the waiting page, the host will be able to change the game settings and add custom categories if desired. Once the host starts the game your trivia board will start generating.

Once the board has been generated a player is then selected to choose a category and price. That player is highlighted in green. (Note that you can scroll up to bring the players list into view)

The chosen clue is then displayed. Any player can buzz-in (either by clicking the button or hitting the spacebar) to answer, but once someone buzzes in everyone else must wait for them to finish answering before being able to buzz-in again. Incorrect answers are penalized, while a correct answer wins you the price! The timer for the clue appears at the bottom, and a separate timer for answering appears when you buzz in.

This continues until there are no more clues left. The player with the highest number of points wins!

Custom Category Tips

Guidelines to follow to have a better experience with custom categories!
  • Be more general than specific. This doesn't mean that being specific is a bad thing! Just try not to make the scope too narrow. For example, instead of saying "Spongebob Squarepants," try saying "Cartoons." Gemini will have a lot more to work with from Wikipedia that way.
  • Limit each category to a single subject. It will make the information retrieval process more streamlined and relevant to the category's overall intent.
  • Refrain from inappropriate/sensitive topics. Gemini will block queries that pertain to such topics and you likely won't have said custom category generate at all. If that happens, however, we can assure you that another category will automatically replace it!

FAQ

Everything looks too big for my screen, how can I adjust it?

Use Ctrl-/Cmd- to zoom out!

How can I see the players in my room and their scores?

You can scroll up to reveal the players and scroll down to hide them.

Why is my custom category not appearing on the board?

Gemini is having trouble creating trivia for your category, this is likely due to an inappropriate category.

Why are the clue and answers not matching the category?

There is likely not enough information available to properly create trivia for the category. Trivial uses Wikipedia as a trusted source of information to create trivia for you.

Why does the board take so long to load?

Generating the board relies on the Gemini api generating a response. The larger the board the more times we would need to call Gemini. Keep in mind we are using the free version of Gemini so bear with us!
